Our Journey

2020 - The Beginning

Founded with a vision to revolutionize STEM education in Pakistan. Started with a small team of passionate educators and engineers committed to making technology accessible to young minds.

2021 - First Programs

Launched our flagship robotics programs for children aged 6-18. Partnered with 5 leading schools in Lahore to introduce structured robotics curriculum.

2022 - Rapid Growth

Expanded to 15+ partner schools and trained over 200 students. Introduced advanced AI and machine learning modules for senior students.

2023 - Innovation Hub

Established our innovation lab and started hosting national robotics competitions. Launched virtual learning programs to reach students nationwide.

2024 - Leading the Future

Now serving 500+ students across 25+ institutions. Recognized as a leading STEM education provider with plans for international expansion.
